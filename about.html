Our Story

How My Online Dabba started and our journey so far

Founded in 2023 by a group of food enthusiasts, My Online Dabba was born out of a desire to share the joy of home-cooked meals with those who lead busy lives. We understand the challenges of finding time to cook nutritious meals every day, and we're here to make it easier without compromising on quality or taste.

Our team is dedicated to sourcing the freshest ingredients and preparing meals that not only taste great but also nourish the body. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect with culture, family, and tradition. That's why every meal we prepare is made with love and attention to detail.

At My Online Dabba, we're more than just a tiffin service; we're a community of food lovers who value the importance of good food in our lives. Join us on this culinary journey and experience the comfort of home-cooked meals delivered to your door.
